How to Play

Welcome to Ocean Drifting! Your goal is to drift across the ocean and discover interesting items.

  • Your ship automatically drifts, increasing your distance traveled
  • Watch for discoveries that appear on the horizon
  • Click "Investigate" to collect discoveries
  • Use "Drop Anchor" to pause your journey
